Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

Promptwise is a browser extension that helps users improve prompts before sending them into supported AI chat websites. This Privacy Policy explains what information Promptwise handles and how it is used.

Information We Process

Promptwise may read the text you type into a supported prompt box so it can analyze and rewrite the prompt. If you paste, upload, or attach an image to the prompt box, Promptwise may temporarily process that image to extract text from it.

How Information Is Used

Prompt text and supported image content are used only to provide Promptwise features, including prompt analysis, prompt rewriting, image text extraction, applying the improved prompt back into the prompt box, and optionally sending the improved prompt.

Local Storage

Promptwise may store limited temporary data in your browser using local extension storage. This can include the most recent prompt image or related prompt state needed for the extension to function properly.

Third-Party Services

Promptwise may send prompt text or image content to an AI service through the extension's background script in order to generate rewritten prompts or extract text from images. Do not enter sensitive personal, financial, medical, or confidential information into Promptwise unless you are comfortable with that information being processed for this purpose.

Information We Do Not Sell

Promptwise does not sell your prompt text, images, or personal information.

User Control

You can choose whether to use Promptwise's automatic prompt interception and rewriting features. You may also disable or remove the extension at any time through your browser's extension settings.

Data Security

Promptwise is designed to process only the information needed to perform its prompt-improvement features. However, no software or online service can guarantee complete security.

Children's Privacy

Promptwise is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
